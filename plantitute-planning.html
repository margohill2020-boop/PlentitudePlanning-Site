FAQ

The questions people ask before they commit.

How is Plantitute different from a traditional financial advisor?

Traditional advisors often focus mainly on investment management and charge an AUM fee. Plantitute Planning covers your full financial life and uses flat-fee pricing instead.

Do you act as a fiduciary?

Yes. This site positions Plantitute as a fiduciary planning firm, focused on objective advice rather than product sales or commissions.

Can couples or families plan together?

Absolutely. The planning framework is designed for households, shared goals, and major life decisions that affect more than one person.

What planning topics are included?

Investing, taxes, retirement, cash flow, real estate, education funding, equity compensation, insurance, estate planning, and related planning needs.
